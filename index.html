As an introduction, my name is Anna Nguyen, and I am currently a third year undergraduate at University of California, Riverside. Due to the COVID restrictions back in 2020, my experience at UCR is very limited as there are multiple of missed opportunities that I was not able to take advantage of. However, now that the campus is fully open to students, I have been actively partaking in research opportunities as well as utilizing on-campus resources that UCR has to offer.

I am the oldest within a family of four, and am a first generation college student that will be receiving a Bachelors of Science in Microbiology in June 2023. As my parents immigrate from Vietnam to America after WWII, they had hoped for a better future and lifestype for their children. As the oldest, it is quite burdesome and stressful to have to be the first of the family; however, it is also a blessing to have this opportunity to grow and find my own indivduality and purpose within society.

In regards to my career aspirations, my goal is to obtain a position as a certified clinical lab scientist, or CLS. The healthcare industry has always piqued my interest throughout high school, but I never understood what part of the healthcare industry that I wanted to partake in. Now that I have experience in a laboritory setting, I realized that I enjoy actively working behind-the-scenes while indirectly partaking in patient diagnostics.
